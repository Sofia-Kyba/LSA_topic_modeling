All levels welcome. Students must have a personal yoga experience to participate in this class. These classes are a combination of yoga sequences, with the intention of building strength, flexibility and ultimately to maintain balance. This class is open to all levels. This is a personal training program that involves a specific workout plan, performed at the highest level of fitness possible. We will work on building the body of a muscular man or woman. You will work your body on all levels to build muscle, strength, and get the most out of your workout. The schedule is 5 x 3 sets of 10 reps with a 30 sec rest between each exercise.
